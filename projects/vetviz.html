Scientific Visualization

This course consisted of several labs within the areas of scalar- and vector visualizations, and volume rendering. Areas covered include higher order integration schemes, iso surfaces, different data grid structures, visualization methods for scalar data fields and vector fields, and rendering (sampling) techniques for volumetric data.

The labs were implemented in C++ and used Visualization Toolkit (VTK) and Voreen (Volume Rendering Engine), and were completed in collaboration with two fellow students.
